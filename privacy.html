Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

What We Collect

AMCOL may collect contact details, company information, route information, cargo details, request submissions, and portal-related information that you choose to provide through this website.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to transport requests, coordinate dispatch, support client communication, improve service delivery, and maintain the security and operation of our website tools.

Sharing

AMCOL does not sell personal information. We may share relevant operational details with staff, service providers, or logistics partners only when needed to deliver support, scheduling, transport coordination, or required compliance activity.

Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect submitted information, but no website or online system can guarantee absolute security. Users should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive data through public forms.

Your Choices

If you need to update, correct, or remove information you submitted through this site, contact AMCOL directly so we can review and assist with the request.

Contact

Privacy or data questions can be directed to AMCOL through the main support line at +1 (868) 399-9200 or from the contact section on the main website.
